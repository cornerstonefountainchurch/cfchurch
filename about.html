Who We Are

Cornerstone Fountain Church is a Bible-believing, Spirit-led community dedicated to building lives through the transformative power of God's Word. We are passionate about discipleship, outreach, and creating a space where all people can grow in faith and purpose.

Our leadership team is committed to serving the church with integrity, vision, and a heart for people.
